Never Fall Behind Again

Upload your missed lecture notes (PDF) or lecture recordings and let Gemma 4 transform them into easy summaries, explanations, quizzes and interactive learning.

The Problem

Thousands of students miss lectures because of illness, transportation problems, financial challenges, family responsibilities or emergencies. Missing one lecture often means struggling to understand future topics.

Our Solution

ClassCatch AI helps students catch up quickly by using Gemma 4 to analyze lecture PDFs and audio recordings. It creates summaries, explains difficult concepts, answers questions and generates revision quizzes.
