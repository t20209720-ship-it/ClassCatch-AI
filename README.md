# 🎓 ClassCatch AI

> Never Fall Behind Again

## 📖 Overview

ClassCatch AI is an AI-powered education assistant built with **Gemma 4**.

It helps students who miss lectures because of illness, transportation problems, financial challenges, family responsibilities, or emergencies.

Students can upload lecture PDFs or audio recordings, and ClassCatch AI transforms them into easy-to-understand summaries, explanations, quizzes, and study notes.

The application is designed to work in low-connectivity environments, making learning accessible even when internet access is limited.

---

# 🚨 The Problem

Every day, thousands of students miss important lectures.

Missing one class often means struggling to understand future lessons.

Existing learning platforms mainly focus on live classes or video recordings and do not provide personalized AI learning support that works well in areas with poor internet connectivity.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

ClassCatch AI uses **Gemma 4** to understand lecture materials and create personalized learning experiences.

Students simply upload a PDF or lecture recording.

The application then generates:

- Lecture summaries
- Simple explanations
- Revision notes
- Quiz questions
- AI-powered learning assistance
